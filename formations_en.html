🧼

Hygiene and Food Safety in the Kitchen

The Hygiene and Food Safety training is offered free of charge to our members, volunteers, and partners. It ensures safety in terms of hygiene and food safety both at the Community Hub and at home.

  • Compliance with MAPAQ regulations
  • Explanatory guide provided to each participant
  • Safe food handling practices
  • Cleaning and disinfection techniques
  • Cross-contamination prevention

📅 Duration: 3 hours

🎯 Audience: Members, volunteers and partners

💵 Cost: Free

📝 Certification: Participation certificate
