Our Mission

The Carrefour of Educational & Entrepreneurial Initiatives is much more than a charity organization. We are a community support system that fights poverty and social exclusion by infusing every action with kindness, compassion, and love. Our holistic approach connects access to basic needs with a comprehensive pathway to autonomy and self-sufficiency.
